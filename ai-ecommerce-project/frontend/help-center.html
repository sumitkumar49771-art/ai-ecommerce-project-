Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? ▾
Go to the My Orders page after logging in to see the live status of every order you've placed.
How do I return or exchange an item? ▾
Visit our Returns & Refunds page for eligibility rules and step-by-step return instructions.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, UPI, and cash on delivery on eligible orders.
How long does delivery take? ▾
Most orders arrive within 3-7 business days. Check our Shipping Info page for details specific to your area.
How do I reset my password? ▾
Click "Forgot Password" on the Login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How does the AI shopping assistant work? ▾
Click the chat icon in the bottom corner of any page to ask questions, get product suggestions, or compare items instantly.

Still Need Help?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is happy to help — visit our Contact Us page, or use the AI Shopping Assistant chat icon for instant answers.
