Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email, phone number, and delivery addresses.
  • Order Information: purchase history, cart items, and wishlist data.
  • Usage Data: pages visited, search queries, and interactions used to power recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process orders, personalize product recommendations, improve our AI shopping assistant, provide customer support, and communicate order updates.

3. AI & Personalization

Our recommendation engine and AI assistant analyze your browsing and purchase behavior to suggest relevant products. This data is used only within ShopAI and is never sold to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers (such as payment processors and delivery partners) strictly to fulfill your orders.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your cart, and understand site usage. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted transactions and secure authentication.

7. Your Rights

  • Access or update your account information at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of promotional communications.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, reach out via our Contact Us page.
