Shipping Charges

We offer free delivery on orders above ₹499. Orders below this amount incur a flat shipping fee of ₹49, calculated automatically at checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you can track it in real time from the My Orders page. You'll also receive status updates at each stage — Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

Delivery Partners

We work with trusted logistics partners across India to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time, including to remote and rural pin codes.

Delays & Issues

Occasionally, weather, regional restrictions, or high order volume can delay delivery. If your order is significantly delayed, please check the order status page first, and reach out via our Contact Us page if you need further help.

International Shipping

At this time, ShopAI ships only within India. International shipping may be introduced in the future.
