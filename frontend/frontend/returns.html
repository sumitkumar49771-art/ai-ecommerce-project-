Returns & Refunds

Not the right fit? Here's how our return and refund process works.

Return Window

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they're unused, in original packaging, and with all tags intact.

📅

7-Day Window

Starts from the date of delivery, not order date.

📦

Original Condition

Unused, unwashed, with tags and packaging intact.

💰

Fast Refunds

Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after pickup.

How to Request a Return

  • Go to My Orders and select the item you want to return.
  • Choose "Return / Exchange" and select a reason.
  • Schedule a pickup, or drop off at the nearest location if applicable.
  • Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund is processed automatically.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned unless defective:

  • Personal care and beauty products that have been opened
  • Innerwear and swimwear
  • Perishable grocery items
  • Customized or personalized products

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Cash-on-delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer or ShopAI wallet credit, whichever you prefer.

Need Help?

If you're facing an issue with a return, visit our Help Center or Contact Us directly.
